Make Your Way Up The Tiers

At certain point brackets, you will reach certain tiers. Each tier comes with a set of benefits, as you progress through the tiers, benefits add up, fast.

Tiers

Bronze

4000 Points

Benefits:

  • 25% discount on any Explore lounge, throughout the Explore Airlines network
  • 5 kilograms of additional baggage allowance
  • 25% discount on onboard Wi-Fi
  • If you complete the personal preference page in your account, we will make note of your preferences for any future flights with Explore Airlines

Silver

10000 Points

Benefits:

  • All the benefits of the Bronze tier plus a few more!
  • An additional 25% discount on any Explore lounge, throughout the Explore Airlines network
  • An additional 5 kilograms of baggage allowance
  • An additonal 25% discount on onboard Wi-Fi
  • Priority baggage delivery, your luggage will be given priority tagging when the flight is over, our employees will hand your baggage over to you, just in front of the baggage carousel

Gold

22000 Points

Benefits:

  • All the benefits of the Silver tier plus a few more!
  • Free entry and access to any Explore lounge, throughout the Explore Airlines network
  • An additional 5 kilograms of baggage allowance
  • Free onboard Wi-Fi
  • Share your benefits! You can make one nomination to upgrade someone to silver tier during your time as a gold member, your nominee will instantly redeem ten-thousand points for the silver tier
