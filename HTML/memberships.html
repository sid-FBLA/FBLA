For every mile you travel, you earn an Explore Point, simple as that.

No catches. No experies. No "blackout dates."

Join our rewards system, and start racking up Explore Points.

Spend 'Em

1 Explore point = 5 cents

Each point you earn is worth 5 cents, you can use your points to buy anything you want in the Explore Airlines network.

Save 'Em

Make Your Way Up The Tiers

At certain point brackets, you will reach certain tiers. Each tier comes with a set of benefits, as you progress through the tiers, benefits add up, fast.

Tiers

Bronze

4000 Explore Points

Benefits:

  • 25% discount on any Explore lounge, throughout the Explore Airlines network
  • 5 kilograms of additional baggage allowance
  • 25% discount on onboard Wi-Fi
  • If you complete the personal preference page in your account, we will make note of your preferences for any future flights with Explore Airlines

Silver

10000 Explore Points

Benefits:

  • All the benefits of the Bronze tier but improved!
  • 50% discount on any Explore lounge, throughout the Explore Airlines network
  • 10 kilograms of baggage allowance
  • 50% discount on onboard Wi-Fi
  • Priority baggage delivery, our employees will hand over your baggage after the flight

Gold

22000 Explore Points

Benefits:

  • All the benefits of the Silver tier, but improved!
  • Free entry and access to any Explore lounge
  • 15 kilograms of baggage allowance
  • Free onboard Wi-Fi
  • You can nominate one additonal person for a free ten thousand points to become silver tier
